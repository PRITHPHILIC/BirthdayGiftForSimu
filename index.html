Dear Simu,

Happy Birthday to the most annoying yet one of the most precious people in my life 🤍

Pata hai, kuch log slowly life ka part ban jaate hain without even realizing it… aur tu unhi mein se ek hai. Pehle random chats thi, phir aadat ban gayi, aur ab tera ek text na aaye toh din ajeeb sa lagta hai.

Tere saath conversations kabhi normal nahi hoti — kabhi faltu arguments, kabhi nonstop gossip, kabhi random VNs, aur kabhi bas bina reason ek dusre ko irritate karna. But honestly, those little moments are what make everything feel special.

Tu shayad notice nahi karti, but tere saath baat karne se bohot baar mera mood theek hua hai. Even on bad days, tera presence somehow makes things lighter. Aur haan, jab tu “main thik hu” bolke topic change karti hai, tab samajh aa jata hai ki madam kuch toh chhupa rahi hai 😭

So just remember this — tujhe har waqt strong banne ki zarurat nahi hai. There’s always someone who genuinely cares about your happiness, your peace, and even your stupid little problems.

I hope iss birthday ke baad tera life aur zyada beautiful ho — less stress, more smiles, more success, aur woh sab kuch jo tu secretly chahti hai. Khud ko underestimate mat kiya kar, because tu jitna sochti hai usse bohot zyada achi hai.

Stay the same dramatic, caring, irritating person because honestly… life would feel boring without that chaos 😭✨

Once again, Happy Birthday in advance, Simu. Always keep smiling.

— Aampanña 🤍
